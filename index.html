Sales by Month, Day of the Week and Hour

.

Reviewing the data we can assess that there is a huge spike on July, being July 10 the day with the most highest amount of revenue. In these days the mayority of the orders came from McDonald's, KFC, Pizza Hut and Burger King.

We can see also that Thursday and Friday are the stronger business days for Talabat

Also, as expected, Talabat's services are not used equal throughout the day. We can see high sales from 1pm to 5pm and then a spike from 7 pm to 9 pm, being 8pm the Talabat's top hour for doing business. After 9pm we can see a decline in ordershitting rock bottom at 4am and 5 am.

Aside from the available data, ¿What other features would you include for an effective segmentation?

.

As some people and managers tend to say: "There is not such thing as too much information", and we can say that within Analytics it is mostly true, specially regarding Ecommerce. There is many and many more information that we can include to the database in order to gain greater insights and informacion about our clients. Analytics can provide Talabat with data-driven insights into how shoppers interact with the site and app — both the good and the bad. These insights take the guesswork and subjectivity out of website and tech optimization, and uncover opportunities for improvement and innovation.

The hard truth that most companies tend to overlook is tha in this digital age we can collect as much data as we want — and use it effectively — but if we dont use it with the purpose of listening to what our customers are saying, we will have a hard time giving them what they are looking for — and they may end up going elsewhere to find it.

.

A few examples of the information that we can include for an effective segmentation are:

Payment Method

Payment analytics can provide insight into how our products are performing in different regions and how different payment types and currencies are contributing the overall revenue.

Based on the this information we can create a plan to expand our reach and overall strategy, supporting international customers better.

Products

Knowing which products do people commonly buy in a particular country or day would can help us understand better our customers behavior and then we can give offers and discounts only on the product that people will mostly prefer to buy rather than giving to it to random products.

.

Demographics

Demographics are a key part of the business and marketing strategy. Demographics help Talabat identify the individual members of the audience by key characteristics, wants and needs.

We can use demographics to refine our marketing budget and make more targeted investment decisions about how to engage our audience.
